Senate Bill S2021

De minimis tax exemption for small-scale Bitcoin sales with a $20,000 yearly exemption. Introduced January 9th by Senator Appollonio.

2025 Legislative Report

Despite S0451 not advancing this session, RIBPI founder Dan Hersey reports significant progress in shifting Rhode Island's approach to blockchain policy. The state has moved from outright dismissal to serious legislative engagement.

Bills Introduced

  • S0373/H5810: Blockchain Study Commission
  • H5564: Economic Growth Blockchain Act
  • H6007: State Treasurer Digital Asset Investment
  • H5868/S0375: Cryptographic Key Protection

The key takeaway: Rhode Island is shifting from dismissal to serious engagement with blockchain technology and Bitcoin policy.
