About RIBPI

A nonpartisan research and advocacy organization committed to shaping smart, forward-looking Bitcoin policy at the state level.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate Rhode Island lawmakers, draft sound legislation, and deliver expert testimony to advance Bitcoin and blockchain policy that fosters innovation while protecting consumers.

What We Do

Three pillars of the Rhode Island Bitcoin Policy Initiative

Education & Outreach

We help Rhode Island lawmakers understand Bitcoin, blockchain technology, and the policy implications of cryptocurrency innovation. Through briefings, one-on-one meetings, and direct engagement, we build the understanding legislators need to make informed decisions.

Drafting Bills & Testimony

We write legislation, prepare fiscal impact analyses, and deliver testimony before committees in the Rhode Island General Assembly. From de minimis tax exemptions to private key protections, we turn policy ideas into actionable bills.

Policy Research

We produce nonpartisan white papers, one-pagers, and briefing documents that help lawmakers evaluate the real-world impacts of Bitcoin and blockchain policy on innovation, consumer protection, and economic development.
