2026 Active Legislation

Four bills we've drafted and introduced in the Rhode Island General Assembly this session

H7956 / S2198
Introduced

Blockchain Study Commission

Creates a legislative commission to study blockchain applications for voting, land registries, public records, and economic development

H7413
Introduced

Economic Growth Blockchain Act

Regulatory sandbox and Special Purpose Depository Institutions to make RI a blockchain innovation hub

H7957 / S2196
Introduced

Private Key Protection

Protects citizens from being compelled to disclose private cryptographic keys in legal proceedings

S2021
Introduced

De Minimis Tax Exemption

Tax-free Bitcoin payments up to $5,000/month ($20,000/year) to reduce friction for everyday use
